Markiplier Age: All About the Gamer-Turned-Movie Producer

Markiplier is one of the most famous internet personalities. The OG YouTuber has been on the platform since 2012 and has helped shape the gaming community into what it is today.

It might surprise you to learn that Mark Fischbach didn’t plan to make a career out of YouTube. Instead, he was only sharing his gaming content with a few friends when bored.

Keep reading to learn about Markiplier’s journey to fame and his future creative endeavors. Let’s dive in!

Markiplier Biography

Mark Edward Fischbach was born on June 28, 1989, in Honolulu, Hawaii. As of 2024, he’s 35 years old. His father is German-American, and his mother is of Korean descent.

When Markiplier was just a toddler, his family relocated to Cincinnati, Ohio. shortly after, his parents got divorced.

Fischbach lived with his mother in Ohio, where he attended Milford High School and attended the University of Cincinnati, where he studied biomedical engineering.

In 2012, Fischbach registered his first YouTube channel to let off steam and share his passion for video games, and the rest was history!

Markiplier Career

At just 23 years old, Mark Fischbach signed up for YouTube. He chose the screen name Markiplier as a play on his name and Multiplier.

Then, Markiplier started uploading playthrough videos, where he’d just film himself playing popular video games. His style and friendly personality quickly catapulted him into popularity. Soon after, he gained thousands of followers and started earning money from his videos.

This took Mark by surprise, who was in no way a professional. So, he decided to quit college in order to focus on his growing online career. With the help of his college roommates, his channel flourished even more.

In 2014, Markiplier uploaded a playthrough video of the indie game Five Nights at Freddy’s. Though the game is now a worldwide sensation, with a movie adaptation, it had been released for only five days when Fischbach picked it up.

Well, Markiplier’s videos were incredibly successful. Next, the then-indie game became a hit, proving online gamers' significant influence. Currently, the first FNaF video has over 100 million views, and fans are calling Mark the King of FNaF!

Other Ventures

There’s no denying that Markiplier has one of the most promising YouTube careers. However, there seems to be no limits to Fischbach’s creative ambitions.

In 2018, Markiplier collaborated with fellow YouTuber and long-time friend Sean McLoughlin, also known as JackSepticEye, to launch a clothing brand, Cloak.

That’s not all. Markiplier has also hosted a variety of YouTube shows and podcasts.

Now, Mark is preparing to make his debut on the big screen. Fischbach has been working on an exciting new project, a feature film. He’s set to direct and star in Iron Lung, based on the video game of the same name.

Markiplier Net Worth

Markiplier is the third-highest-paid content creator on YouTube. He has an estimated net worth of $45 million.

However, YouTube isn’t the only way Fischbach earns his living. His clothing brand is also pretty lucrative, and he even has an exclusive OnlyFans where he shares photoshoots and behind-the-scene footage.

Final Thoughts

Markiplier’s age surprises many fans. After all, it’s difficult to imagine how a 23-year-old was responsible for creating the online gaming community.

Millions of fans await Mark’s playthrough videos. Not to mention the thousands rewatching all his entertaining content from years ago, especially his Five Nights at Freddy’s series.

Now, at 35, Markiplier is a multi-millionaire, exploring numerous creative outlets, including film.
